Privacy Policy for Compresser

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Introduction

Compresser ("we", "our", "the app") is a file compression application for Android. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Compresser does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All file compression operations are performed entirely on your device.

Files You Compress

All files (images, videos, audio, PDFs) you select for compression are processed locally on your device. We never upload your files to any server.

Advertising

The app may display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policya>.

Analytics

We may use anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Permissions

The app requests access to device storage solely to read files you choose to compress and to save compressed output files.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
