Compresser — Support

Compresser is an offline-first compression tool for images, videos, audio files and PDFs on iOS and Android. Everything runs on your device — your files never leave it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All compression is performed locally on your device. Compresser does not have a backend and never transmits the content of your files.

Which formats are supported?

  • Images: JPEG, PNG, HEIC, WebP
  • Videos: MP4, MOV, M4V
  • Audio: MP3, M4A, WAV
  • Documents: PDF

How do I change compression quality?

Open the app, tap Settings, and choose a quality preset (Light, Balanced, Strong) or a custom bitrate for videos.

Where are compressed files saved?

On iOS, compressed photos and videos are saved to your Photos library or Files app, depending on your selection. On Android, files go to your chosen folder via the system picker.

How do I remove ads?

The current release is free and ad-supported. A paid ad-free tier is planned for a future update.

How do I report a bug?

Please email mail@macsiem.dev with:

  • Device model and OS version
  • Compresser app version (visible in Settings → About)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • A brief description of the expected vs actual result
